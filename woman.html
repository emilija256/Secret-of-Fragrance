Dolce & Gabbana the one

Dolce & Gabbana The One Eau de Parfum is a fragrance with a strong character, intended for a woman who knows who she is and what she stands for, to a woman of strong character. Fruity, floral fragrance with oriental notes.

The fragrance has a fruity beginning of bergamot, mandarin, lychee and peach, which offers a smooth transition into a floral heart consisting of lily of the valley, white lily and plum. The base is woody powdery with accords of vetiver, amber, musk and vanilla. A warm sensual fragrance for a woman who does not shy away from the spotlight.

90 €

Chanel Gabrielle

Gabrielle by Chanel is a very luxurious, refined, intriguing and irresistible women's perfume. Chanel Gabrielle perfume is inspired by the daring and rebellious character of Gabrielle Coco Chanel and her youthful years before she became famous.

Gabrielle perfume opens with notes of mandarin, grapefruit and blackcurrant. At the heart of this sophisticated, enchanting, glamorous and attractive perfume are tuberose, ylang-ylang, jasmine and orange blossom, while the base of the perfume consists of a blend of sandalwood and musk notes.

120 €

My Burberry

My Burberry by Burberry is a floral fragrance for women. My Burberry was launched in 2014. The perfume was created by Francis Kurkdjian.

Top notes are pea flower, bergamot, mandarin orange, grapefruit and lemon; middle notes are quince, white freesia, geranium, peach, green notes, gardenia and passionfruit; base notes are damask rose, rose, patchouli, musk, leather and violet.

95 €

Victoria's Secret bombshell

Tis the season for enchantment. Joyful and bright, Bombshell is an extension of the American fragrance. Number 1. Inspired by the holidays, this perfume interweaves decadent fruit with swirling petals to reveal an intoxicating signature.

The fragrance opens with a rush of blackberry elixir, a fruity pop against a floral heart of sparkling starry peony. Basically, a lush combination of vanilla and patchouli envelops the fragrance in a warm, woody embrace.

60 €

Narciso Rodriguez

The story of sensuality and seduction continues with a new chapter, a velvety, at the same time powerful and delicate, warm and strong Narciso Poudrée fragrance. The goal was to create a mince cloud that highlights natural, mature femininity.

Powdery musk dominates the heart of the perfume. which opens with a floral combination of Bulgarian rose and jasmine. Woody notes of vetiver, black and white cedar complete the composition.

Aqua Allegoria Florabloom

Aqua Allegoria Florabloom by Guerlain is a floral fragrance for women and men. This is a new perfume. Aqua Allegoria Florabloom was launched in 2024. The perfume was created by Delphine Jelk. Top notes are mango, bergamot and lime; middle notes are tuberose, iris, violet and rose; base notes are coconut, musk and sandalwood.

70 €

Chanel Coco Mademoiselle

Chanel Coco Mademoiselle is a fragrance that perfectly balances sophistication with modern boldness. Its dynamic composition opens with refreshing citrus notes of orange and bergamot, energizing and invigorating. As the scent develops, floral tones of jasmine and rose transition into a rich heart, adding a sophisticated femininity that is both delicate and strong. Base notes of patchouli, vanilla, and amber provide depth and warmth, creating a layered and long-lasting impression.

This fragrance is crafted for women who want to make a strong statement and follow their inner strength with style. It's ideal for everyday wear as well as special occasions when you want to stand out. Coco Mademoiselle is more than just a scent – it’s a declaration. With it, every woman can express her unique personality, feel confident, and irresistibly attractive, regardless of the circumstances.

150 €

Flowerbomb by Viktor & Rolf

Flowerbomb by Viktor&Rolf is an iconic Oriental Floral fragrance that has captivated women since its launch in 2005. Created by renowned perfumers Olivier Polge, Carlos Benaim, Domitille Michalon Bertier, and Dominique Ropion, this scent is a masterful blend of sophistication and intensity.

The fragrance opens with a fresh and invigorating burst of tea, bergamot, and osmanthus, setting a lively and elegant tone. As it evolves, the heart reveals a rich bouquet of orchids, jasmine, roses, freesia, and African orange flower, enveloping the wearer in a luxurious and opulent floral symphony. The base notes of patchouli, musk, and vanilla ground the composition, adding a sensual and warm finish that lingers enticingly.

Flowerbomb is not just a perfume; it's a sensory experience that embodies the essence of femininity and strength. It is designed for the woman who embraces her individuality and desires to leave a memorable impression. With its captivating blend of floral and oriental elements, Flowerbomb exudes an aura of glamour and allure, making it a perfect choice for both everyday wear and special occasions.

130 €

Dahlia Divin by Givenchy

Dahlia Divin by Givenchy is a sophisticated amber-floral fragrance for women, introduced in 2014. Created by renowned perfumer François Demachy, this scent epitomizes luxury and elegance.

The top notes open with a rich blend of Mirabelle plum, citrus accords, and pink pepper, creating a fresh and fruity introduction. The heart of the fragrance is lush and complex, featuring exquisite tones of jasmine Sambac, peach, orange blossom, rose, black currant, apple, and lily of the valley. This floral symphony brings sophistication and depth to the scent, enveloping the wearer in a luxurious and feminine aura.

The base notes provide a perfect finishing touch with a rich combination of patchouli, sandalwood, vetiver, vanilla, white musk, and cedar. These notes add warmth, depth, and longevity, creating an unforgettable impression.

Dahlia Divin is designed for women who seek a sophisticated and irresistible fragrance that expresses their inner strength and elegance. It is ideal for special occasions and evening outings, where it can leave a powerful and lasting impression.

180 €
