Givenchy gentlemen only

Givenchy Gentleman Only - a distinctly masculine, elegant, attractive and attractive perfume intended for modern men today.

Gentlemen Only opens with notes of green mandarin, pink pepper, nutmeg and birch leaf. At the heart of this refined, charming and irresistible perfume are cedar, patchouli and vetiver, while the base of the perfume consists of a mixture of incense and musk notes.

Versace eros flame

Versace eros flame is a fragrance for a strong, passionate, self-confident man who knows his emotions intimately. At the same time, he notices the current feelings and enjoys the eternal fragrance of true love, transporting us from the real world to the mythological universe, the home of heroes who turn their flaws into virtues.

The top notes are especially bright. Fresh and juicy Italian citrus such as lemon, tangerine and bitter orange are warmed by notes of black pepper from Madagascar and wrapped in aromatic wild rosemary. Through the olfactory wave of pepperwood, geranium and rose, we reach the bottom notes in which noble woods such as Texas cedar, patchouli, Haitian vetiver, mountain oakmoss accord and sandalwood are subtly mixed, creating a soft and sensual whole with tonka berries that perfectly intertwine with vanilla.

150 €

Guy Laroche Drakkar noir

Drakkar Noir is a distinguished, aromatic fougere fragrance for a strong man with a natural, seductive appeal. An explosive burst of fragrant chords is present in honor of the fearless and brave ancient Vikings and their battleship, the drekar. The Drakkar Noir man is the heir to their strength, evoking their spirit with a powerful scent that is chosen to win his day.

Drakkar Noir opens with fresh notes of lemon and tangerine, with a texture of coriander and juniper spices. Warm, herbaceous tones of basil and rosemary evoke the heart of the forest, while cedar and vetiver form a sensual finish. A refined masculine fragrance with an intense, long-lasting trail. Fragrance family: fougere Top notes: wormwood and coriander Middle notes: juniper, geranium, lavender Bottom notes: moss, patchouli, fir balsam.

40 €

Healy Mood

Healy Mood is one of the perfumes offered by the Healy brand, known for its philosophy of combining scent notes with emotional and mental benefits. Healy Mood is a perfume designed to stimulate positive emotions and improve mood. Its composition combines harmonious fragrance notes that provide a feeling of relaxation and satisfaction.

With carefully selected ingredients, this perfume is ideal for everyday use, giving you a sense of grace and balance throughout the day.

Davidoff Cool Water

Davidoff Cool Water is a classic men’s fragrance launched in 1988, known for its refreshing and energetic character. The scent opens with bright citrus notes of bergamot and lemon, enhanced by mint and lavender, creating a sensation of purity and freshness. The heart reveals a richness of floral and spicy tones, including jasmine, pink pepper, and coriander, adding complexity and depth.

Base notes of sandalwood, vetiver, and musk provide a lasting woody and musky finish, leaving a subtle and sophisticated trail. Davidoff Cool Water is ideal for everyday wear, offering a sense of vitality and elegance whether worn during warmer months or on any occasion.

95 €

Valentino Born in Roma Uomo

Valentino Born in Roma Uomo is a modern and sophisticated men’s fragrance introduced in 2019. Created by the perfumery team including Antoine Maisondieu, Olivier Cresp, and Alberto Morillas, this scent reflects the urban spirit and luxurious character of the Valentino brand.

The fragrance opens with refreshing and spicy notes of bergamot, black pepper, and ginger, providing a vibrant and dynamic start. The heart of the perfume reveals rich notes of lavender, sage, and floral elements, adding layers of complexity and sophistication. The base notes are warm and powerful, featuring woody amber, tonka bean, and vanilla, which leave a lasting and seductive trail.

Valentino Born in Roma Uomo is perfect for modern men who want to express their style and confidence. Its complex and harmonious composition makes it suitable for any occasion, whether for everyday activities or special events, always delivering a sense of luxury and elegance.

85 €

Versace Man Eau Fraiche

Versace Man Eau Fraîche is a sophisticated and refreshing fragrance designed for modern men seeking an elegant and energetic scent. Launched in 2006, this perfume belongs to the citrus-woody family, making it ideal for daily wear.

Top notes are very refreshing and include lemon, bergamot, and pink pepper, which immediately give a sense of clarity and energy. Middle notes feature spicy and floral elements such as nutmeg, lavender, and woody accords, adding depth and complexity to the fragrance. Base notes are warm and woody, with hints of amberwood, musk, and vetiver, providing a long-lasting and sophisticated finish.

Overall, Versace Man Eau Fraîche is an elegant, refreshing, and understated scent, perfect for everyday occasions and warm weather. Its sophisticated yet subtle character makes it a versatile choice for any man who wants to leave an impression with minimal effort.

65 €
